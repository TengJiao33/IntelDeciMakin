## Coding Guide

- `learned object`: what the paper actually learns.
- `structure scope`: whether the model family is fixed before learning or adaptively selected.
- `benchmark/data`: whether data are synthetic, assignment examples, real sorting tasks, ranking proxies, or generic tabular ordinal data.
- `APSS collision risk`: what part of APSS the paper already covers.
- `APSS differentiation`: what APSS could still contribute after acknowledging this paper.
